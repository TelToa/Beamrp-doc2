Getting Started

Getting Whitelisted

In order to get whitelisted into the BeamRP server, you must complete the Whitelist application. After you submit the form, a staff member will look at your application and notify you if you passed or failed. It may take them a while for them to get to your application, please be patient.

Joining the server

In the BeamMP server menu, search for “BeamRP” and join one of the Roleplay or Freeroam servers.

Bot commands

BeamRP uses a Discord bot for commands, the following are the commands you can use in #bot-commands


/initalize

Initalizes you into the system, use it to make your first character, or to make a new character for when you die. You will start with $5,000

Arguments:

user: Your BeamMP username

Example:

/initialize

/status

Shows your balance, license, and blacklist status

Arguments:

User

Examples:

/status
Returns balance, license, and blacklist status of yourself

/status @itsaname
Returns balance, license, and blacklist status of itsaname


/addmoney

Adds money to your balance

Arguments:

Amount: The amount of money to add

Reason: The reason to add money

Examples:

/addmoney 200 Two deliveries
Gives your $200 for doing two deliveries

/addmoney 700 Got paid by @gitty
adds $700 that were given from gitty (make sure they subtract their own money first)


/delmoney

Removes money from your balance

Arguments:

Amount: The amount of money to remove

Reason: The reason to remove money

Examples:

/delmoney 6000 Bought ETK K-Series KC4 (M)
Removes $6,000, lets the bot know you spent your money on buying an ETK

/delmoney 15000 Paying fines
Removes $15,000, which you spent on paying fines

/delmoney 700 Paid @itsaname
Removes the money you paid @itsaname (doesn't automatically pay them)

Section 3
Vehicles

Your first vehicle

You're ready to buy your first vehicle! You only have $5,000, so you can't buy anything fancy yet. You can filter the price of vehicles in the vehicle spawn menu by going to the filters section on the right, opening the "value" section, and setting the maximum price you are looking for.
Note: check "Expand Configurations" to see all vehicles that fit your range

Each vehicle has a tier, consider the tier of the vehicle you are buying, as it effects the price of insurance and the cost of parts.

Once you have decided which vehicle you want to buy, go to #bot-commands and use the /delmoney command to remove the money from your balance. Make sure to include the vehicle you're buying in the reason section.

Insuring your vehicle

Insurance will cover part of your bill whenever you get into an accident or crash, however you will lose your current insurance if you get into a crash. Insurance cost depends on the tier of your vehicle based on the following rates:
Tier Price
Low tier $500
Mid tier $850
High tier $1000
+High tier $1250
Classics $650

Insurance applies for the day that your are playing, and will have to be re-bought the next day

Modifying your vehicle

In order to modify your vehicle, check the price of the part(s) you want to buy in the parts price spreadsheet. You don't have to go to a repair shop for repair, however for roleplay purposes it is recommended to go to either a shop or garage.

There are no prices for modded cars currently, so use your best judgement

Repairing your vehicle

Reparing your vehicle require you to be able to drive to a repair shop and purchase the part(s) that were broken.

Neither major nor frame damage can be repaired. If your vehicle has major damage, you must "restore" it.

Restoring your vehicle

You can restore damaged vehicles in two ways:
  1. By yourself:
    2. You are only able to restore a vehicle yourself if you can transport the new parts to your vehicle.
    Drive your vehicle to the auto repair shop, or a repair shop you have permission to restore in.
    Buy all the parts you need, and transport them from the harbor to your vehicle.
  2. Paying a company:
    3. Restoration companies get a 50% discount on parts.
    The total cost will include labor fees for the restoration.
